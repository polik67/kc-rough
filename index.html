Kingdom’s Calling is a cooperative action game where four heroes who have amazing elemental abilities (fire, lightning, earth and wind) take on a horde of skeletons commanded by an evil Lich king in an attempt to cleanse the land and restore the kingdom to its former glory. The game takes place on an island that has fallen to ruin, with open plains with destroyed homes, a corrupted graveyard and a crumbling castle; home of the evil Lich.

In Kingdom’s Calling the four heroes are the Assassin, Paladin, Warrior and Archer. The Assassin has the lightning element and pierces her enemies with blue lightning, the Warrior wields the fire element causing massive amounts of damage on all who oppose him, the Paladin hold the earth element, he is the anchor of the group and uses his power to keep his friends safe and last but not least, the Archer who uses the power of wind to support her allies in an attempt to defeat the skeleton horde and stop the mighty Lich that has decimated the lands.

Features include Elemental combo attacks each with different effects, Exhilarating combat with diverse and interesting places to explore, Four characters with unique designs and different elements, Each character has a unique set of characteristics, abilities, attacks and fighting style.
